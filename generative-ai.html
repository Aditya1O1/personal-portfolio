Amazon Customer Reviews Sentiment Analysis

Built a sentiment analysis classifier for Amazon customer reviews using two distinct NLP approaches: VADER (NLTK) and RoBERTa (Transformers by Hugging Face). The project aimed to evaluate and compare the effectiveness of rule-based and deep learning-based models on real-world textual data.

  • Processed thousands of customer reviews using text preprocessing and tokenization pipelines.
  • Implemented VADER for quick lexicon-based sentiment scoring.
  • Fine-tuned RoBERTa transformer for high-accuracy deep learning classification.
  • Compared model performance across precision, recall, and F1-score metrics to determine robustness.
